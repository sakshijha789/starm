Calculate Your Body Mass Index (BMI)

Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.

BMI Categories:

Severe Thinness - 16

Moderate Thinness - 16 to 17

Mild Thinness - 17 to 18.5

Normal - 18.5 to 25

Overweight - 25 to 30

Obese Class I - 30 to 35

Obese Class II - 35 to 40

Obese Class III - 40

Interpretation of the BMI Index

  • Medical management for obesity is recommended under supervision of an Endocrinologist upto a BMI of 32.5.
  • Bariatric and Metabolic surgery should be considered as a treatment option for obesity in people with Asian ethnicity with a BMI more than 37.5 kg/m2 with or without co-morbidities.
  • Bariatric and Metabolic surgery should be considered as a treatment option for obesity in people with Asian ethnicity above a BMI of 32.5 along with at least two of the additional criteria for metabolic syndrome.
