About STARM

We carefully manage the treatment of patients

STARM is a platform for comprehensive and therapeutic obesity education and counseling starm (Behavior feeding activities sport treatment). STARM (Style Training Activity Regimen Management) The Starm platform was founded in 2023 and is a group that includes a team of doctors, specialists and trainers specialized in the treatment and prevention of obesity and overweight.

Starm vision

Our Vision has to provide the best treatment and services to our patients.

We seek to be the leading platform for remote care of obesity and overweight patients by providing integrated consultations, health education, and psychological and physical awareness.
